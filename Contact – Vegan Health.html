The mission of VeganHealth.org is to provide reliable, evidence-based recommendations for the vegan community. Unfortunately, we don’t have the resources to provide personal counseling.

The Vegetarian Nutrition Dietetic Practice Group provides a list of vegan-friendly dietitians who provide personal counseling services and we especially recommend VeganHealth contributor Taylor Wolfram, MS, RDN, LDN.

If you have input about a topic on the site, please comment on a relevant article. Due to limited time, we’re only able to approve and respond to selected comments.
